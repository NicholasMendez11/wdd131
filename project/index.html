What is the Keto Diet?

The ketogenic diet is a low-carb, high-fat eating approach that puts your body into a metabolic state called ketosis. When you drastically reduce carbohydrates, your body becomes incredibly efficient at burning fat for energy instead of glucose.

Unlike other diets, keto focuses on whole foods like meats, fish, eggs, nuts, healthy oils, avocados, and plenty of low-carb vegetables. You'll feel satisfied while your body naturally burns fat for fuel.
