Your Complete Keto Food Guide

Simple lists to help you navigate what to eat and what to avoid

‚úÖ Foods to Enjoy

Proteins

  • Beef, pork, lamb, venison
  • Chicken, turkey, duck
  • Fish and seafood (salmon, tuna, shrimp)
  • Eggs (whole, not just whites)
  • Bacon and sausage (check ingredients)

Healthy Fats

  • Avocados and avocado oil
  • Coconut oil and coconut butter
  • Butter and ghee
  • Olive oil and olives
  • Nuts and seeds (almonds, macadamias, chia)

Low-Carb Vegetables

  • Leafy greens (spinach, kale, lettuce)
  • Cruciferous vegetables (broccoli, cauliflower)
  • Zucchini and cucumber
  • Bell peppers and asparagus
  • Mushrooms and celery

Dairy (if tolerated)

  • Full-fat cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, cream cheese)
  • Heavy cream and sour cream
  • Greek yogurt (full-fat, plain)

‚ùå Foods to Avoid

Grains and Starches

  • Bread, pasta, rice, cereal
  • Oats, quinoa, couscous
  • Corn and corn products
  • Crackers, cookies, cakes

Sugars and Sweeteners

  • Sugar, honey, maple syrup, agave
  • Candy, chocolate (regular), desserts
  • Soft drinks and fruit juices
  • Most condiments with added sugar

High-Carb Vegetables

  • Potatoes, sweet potatoes, yams
  • Carrots and parsnips (in large amounts)
  • Peas and corn

Most Fruits

  • Bananas, apples, oranges
  • Grapes, mangoes, pineapple
  • Dried fruits
  • Small portions of berries are okay

Unhealthy Fats

  • Vegetable oils (soybean, corn, canola)
  • Margarine and trans fats
  • Processed foods with hydrogenated oils
