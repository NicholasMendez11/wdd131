Keto Lifestyle - Website Planning Document

Site Name

Keto Lifestyle Hub

Why I chose this name: I wanted something that feels welcoming and helpful - like a friendly gathering place where people can get real answers about keto. "Hub" makes it sound like the go-to spot where you can find everything you need, whether you're just curious about keto or already living the lifestyle.

Optional domain: ketolifestylehub.com

Site Purpose

Since I've been doing keto for about a month and seeing real results, I want to create a place where people can get honest, practical help with this lifestyle. Here's what I'm planning to share:

  • Simple explanations of what keto actually is (without all the confusing science jargon)
  • Easy-to-understand lists of what you can and can't eat
  • Quick recipes that don't require fancy ingredients or hours in the kitchen
  • Real-world tips for meal prep and eating out without breaking your diet
  • Answers to all those questions everyone asks when they hear about keto
  • My personal experiences - the good, the challenging, and what actually works

I want to make keto feel doable for anyone, whether you're just starting out or looking for fresh ideas to keep going.

User Scenarios

Scenario 1:

"I'm completely new to keto and feeling overwhelmed. What foods can I actually eat, and what should I avoid? I need a simple list to get started."

Scenario 2:

"I've been trying keto for a week but I'm running out of meal ideas. Do you have any easy recipes that don't require complicated ingredients or hours of cooking?"

Scenario 3:

"I want to try keto but I eat out frequently for work. How can I stick to keto when dining at restaurants or ordering takeout?"

Scenario 4:

"I keep hearing conflicting information about keto. Is it really safe? What are the common side effects I should know about?"

Color Schema

Primary Color: Deep Forest Green (#2D5016)

Where I'll use it: Main headings, menu links, and important buttons

Why this color: Green feels healthy and natural - it reminds me of all the fresh vegetables and leafy greens that are such a big part of keto eating

Secondary Color: Warm Cream (#F5F5DC)

Where I'll use it: Page backgrounds and behind content sections

Why this color: It's warm and cozy - like the feeling you get when you're comfortable in your own kitchen trying new recipes

Accent Color: Golden Orange (#FF8C00)

Where I'll use it: To highlight important tips and when you hover over buttons

Why this color: It's energetic and makes me think of delicious food - perfect for grabbing attention on the good stuff

Text Color: Charcoal Gray (#333333)

Where I'll use it: For all the main text you'll be reading

Why this color: It's easy on the eyes but still clear to read - nobody wants to strain their eyes while learning about keto!

Typography

Primary Font: 'Poppins' (Headings)

Where I'll use it: All the big headings, menu items, and buttons

Why I picked it: It looks modern and friendly - not too formal, but still professional enough that people trust the information

Secondary Font: 'Open Sans' (Body Text)

Where I'll use it: All the regular text, lists, and smaller details

Why I picked it: Super easy to read on screens, and it stays clear whether you're on your phone or computer

Accent Font: 'Playfair Display' (Special Elements)

Where I'll use it: Recipe names, inspiring quotes, and special highlights

Why I picked it: It's a bit fancy and adds some personality - perfect for making recipe titles feel special and memorable
